# Editor Meta-Review

Use this after all reviewer reports and the conflict log exist.

## Inputs

- reviewer reports
- conflict log
- journal rubric or user priorities

## Output Sections

### Recommendation

One of:
- `accept`
- `minor_revision`
- `major_revision`
- `reject`

### Rationale

Short paragraph covering:
- novelty
- rigor
- evidence strength
- clarity
- reproducibility
- significance

### Major Revisions

Ranked list of the decision-driving issues.

### Minor Revisions

Ranked list of non-blocking but important improvements.

### Questions For The Authors

Only include questions whose answers could materially change the decision or revision burden.

### Decision Letter

Short editor-style summary that does not lose reviewer disagreement where it matters.
